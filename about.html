🌐 Project Overview

EduAssist is a simple and user-friendly web platform designed to help college students easily find, check eligibility, and apply for scholarships and education loans — all in one place. Our goal is to make financial support for education more accessible to everyone.

🎯 The Problem We Noticed

Many students miss valuable scholarships because of scattered information, eligibility confusion, and lack of guidance during the application process. As first-year students, we saw our peers struggling to find the right scholarships, and that inspired us to create this platform.

💡 What Makes EduAssist Unique

  • ✅ Built-in eligibility checker to match students with suitable scholarships.
  • 🎓 Detailed step-by-step apply guide for every listed scholarship.
  • 📄 Ready-to-download document formats and official links.
  • 💬 Helpful video tutorials and FAQs for common issues.
